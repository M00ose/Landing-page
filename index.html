Feed, Nourish, Empower!
Our mission is to feed and nourish Filipino children and engage the country in the fight to end hunger and malnutrition.
How we make a difference
We envision every Filipino child healthy, nourished and empowered to reach their maximum potential for growth and learning.
We work with kids who have very little or no access to nutritious food due to their condition
Every meal we serve is fortified with a balance of nutrients, designed specially for the needs of undernourished school age children.
We engage every person we know - our friends, our families, everyone in our network in the fight to end hunger and malnutrition in the Philippines.
"I want to pay it forward. Our country is faced with various issus that needs to be resolved and one of those is hunger. It is something that we collect water or noodles from fellowmen with a good heart, but it is another thing that there are people who are willing to physically face our brothers and sisters to feed them and show that there are people who genuinely care.... that's Reach Out & Feed philippines, and I support their goal."
- Joyce Evangelista, Donor
